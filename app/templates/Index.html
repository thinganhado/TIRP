Welcome to ClassForge

Management

Add or remove students, and adjust the number of classes. You can change class sizes or the overall distribution of students, ensuring that the allocation meets your needs.

Visualization

See visual representations of the classroom allocation at both the global (whole school) and individual student level. This includes key factors such as social network analysis, student relationships, and how these impact the allocation.

Customization

Adjust factors like academic balance, social needs, and constraints to customize the system according to your requirements. Interact with the AI chatbot to provide inputs on changes and get real-time feedback on how these changes will affect the allocation.
